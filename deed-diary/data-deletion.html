How to Delete Your Data

Deed Diary stores all data locally on your device only. No data is stored on any server.

To delete all your data immediately:

  1. Open your Android device Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Deed Diary
  3. Tap "Clear Data" to delete all app data, OR
  4. Tap "Uninstall" to remove the app and all its data

Data Types Deleted

  • All deed logs (including mood scores and reflections)
  • Prayer logs
  • Dhikr counts and custom dhikr items
  • Quran bookmarks
  • App settings and preferences
  • Writing practice history

Retention Period

Data is deleted immediately upon uninstall or Clear Data. No backup copies exist on any server.
