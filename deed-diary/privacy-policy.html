Privacy Policy — Deed Diary

Last updated: June 2026
Developer: Indra Settyo (isettyo)
Contact: isettyo@outlook.com

Deed Diary ("the app") is an Islamic lifestyle companion app for tracking good deeds, reading Quran, and practicing Arabic writing. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

1. Data We Store on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only. It is never sent to our servers, never shared with third parties, and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

  • Good deeds you log (category, quantity, optional note, optional mood score, optional reflection text)
  • Prayer logs (completed prayers, sunnah options)
  • Dhikr counts and custom dhikr items
  • Quran bookmarks and memorization (hifz) goals/progress
  • Your manually selected city for prayer times (city, region, country, approximate coordinates) — matched against a city list bundled with the app, never sent to a server
  • App settings (language, font size, selected reciter, last opened page)
  • Writing practice session data

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name or email address, and we do not have user accounts or login systems
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • Your deeds, Quran reading, dhikr, prayer, and writing-practice data never leave your device and are never visible to us

As described in Section 4 below, we do use Firebase to collect limited, anonymous diagnostic and usage data — this is the only data that leaves your device.

3. Location & Qibla Direction

The app requests approximate location permission to calculate accurate Islamic prayer times and Qibla direction for your area using a local algorithm — no location data is sent to any server. You may deny this permission and instead manually select your city from a bundled list (Section 1); prayer times and Qibla direction are then calculated from that selection.

The Qibla Finder also uses your device's compass/magnetometer sensor to show the live direction to the Ka'bah. This sensor reading is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

4. Analytics & Crash Reporting (Firebase)

The app uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to help us understand which features are used and to diagnose crashes or errors so we can fix them.

  • Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage events (e.g. which screens are opened, which features are used) and a non-personal, pseudonymous device/installation identifier. It does not collect your name, email, or any of your deed, Quran, dhikr, or prayer data
  • Firebase Crashlytics collects crash and error reports (e.g. stack traces, device model, OS version) to help us identify and fix bugs
  • This data is processed by Google in accordance with the Firebase Privacy and Security policy

5. Subscription (Pro)

If you purchase Deed Diary Pro, the transaction is processed by Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We use RevenueCat to verify your subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier and your purchase receipt to confirm Pro access. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

We do not receive your payment details. We do not store your subscription status on any server — it is verified directly between your device and RevenueCat.

6. Quran Audio Streaming

When you play verse audio, the audio is streamed from a third-party CDN. Your IP address may be visible to the CDN as part of the standard HTTP request. No personal data is sent.

7. Notifications

The app can schedule local reminders on your device — for example, memorization (hifz) session reminders. These notifications are scheduled and triggered entirely on your device using your device's operating system. No reminder data is sent to or stored on any server.

8. Microphone (Future Feature)

A future version of the app will offer an optional recitation validation feature that uses the microphone. This permission will be requested only when you choose to use that feature. Audio will not be stored or transmitted without your explicit action.

9. Children's Privacy

Deed Diary is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Data Deletion

All app data is stored on your device. To delete all data, uninstall the app. You may also clear individual data types from within the app's Settings screen.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as new features are added. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
